About

I am a PhD student at Virginia Tech interested in the relationship between people, technology, design, and meaningful human experience. My academic work is shaped by curiosity, careful research, and a commitment to building systems that respect the people who use them.

I am also shaped by my Caribbean background, my love for story and culture, and the life I am building with my wife. I keep those parts of myself present in a professional way because they influence how I collaborate, communicate, and approach research.
